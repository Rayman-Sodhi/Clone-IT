Shadow Fight 2 is a role-playing fighting game developed by Russian Cyprus-based developer Nekki. It is the second installment in the Shadow Fight series and was soft-launched on October 22, 2013. The full game was released worldwide on May 1, 2014, for both the Android and iOS operating systems.
Shadow, explains that he was searching for a worthy opponent when he came across the Gates of Shadows, a pathway to the Shadow World. When he opens the Gates, the 6 demons that were imprisoned on the other side were released and Shadow's body was enveloped by the mystical Shadow Energy, which turned him into a silhouette. Believing that sealing the Gates will allow him to regain his body, Shadow set out to defeat the demons and retrieve the seals they are guarding, which can be used to close the Gates.

Shadow participates in various tournaments and battles to prepare himself for the fights with the demons and their bodyguards. The six demons that Shadow has to face are, in order:
LYNX, the leader of an assassin group called The Order
HERMIT, a powerful wizard and the headmaster of a fighting & magic school
BUTCHER, the leader of a juvenile gang
WASP, the Pirate Queen and daughter of the former Pirate King

THE DEADLY BOSSES!!

Each boss has a shield and the players must destroy it within a given time frame before they can finish the boss. After all the shield is destroyed, players can finish the boss in a final one-on-one battle. If they lose the final fight, they can repeat it until they win. Each raid fight consists of one round, lasting 45 seconds. Players can buy or obtain several items like Charges of Darkness and Elixirs to aid them to deal additional damage or survive longer.
